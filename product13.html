Balaclava/Scoodia

Stay warm and stylish with the Balaclava/Scoodia, a versatile and functional accessory designed to provide maximum comfort and protection in cold weather. This piece combines the traditional balaclava with the coziness of a hoodie, making it a must-have for those chilly days.

Materials: Expertly crafted using high-quality yarn, this balaclava/scoodia features a classic white and black color scheme. The stark contrast between the crisp white and deep black not only creates a sleek, modern look but also ensures that it pairs easily with a wide range of winter outfits.

Features:

  • Full Coverage: Designed to cover the head, neck, and lower face, this balaclava/scoodia offers full protection from the elements while maintaining breathability.
  • Adjustable Fit: The flexible yarn ensures a snug yet comfortable fit, while the hood-like design allows for easy adjustments, whether you prefer to wear it fully up or slightly relaxed.
  • Monochrome Style: The white and black color combination is timeless and versatile, making it a perfect match for any winter wardrobe.

Time to Completion: Each Balaclava/Scoodia is carefully crocheted over 3-4 days, ensuring a high level of detail and craftsmanship.

Care Instructions: To maintain the quality and shape of your balaclava/scoodia, hand wash in cold water and lay flat to dry. Avoid using hot water or harsh detergents.
