Ocean Eyes Ruffle Hat

Experience the calming beauty of the ocean with the Ocean Eyes Ruffle Hat. This stunning hat features a layered ruffle design that captures the serene essence of the sea, making it a perfect accessory for those who love a touch of nautical elegance.

Materials: Made with high-quality yarn, the hat showcases a soothing blend of blue, white, and sky blue hues. The deep blue represents the depths of the ocean, the white mimics the crests of the waves, and the sky blue evokes the clear, open sky above.

Features:

  • Ocean-Inspired Ruffles: The delicate ruffles cascade gracefully, reminiscent of gentle ocean waves, adding both texture and movement to your outfit.
  • Soothing Color Palette: The combination of blue, white, and sky blue creates a tranquil and refreshing aesthetic, perfect for bringing a piece of the ocean with you wherever you go.
  • Comfort and Style: Crafted with soft, breathable yarn, this hat offers both comfort and a stylish flair, making it ideal for all-day wear.

Time to Completion: Each Ocean Eyes Ruffle Hat is meticulously crocheted over 3-4 days to ensure every ruffle is perfectly formed and the color transitions are seamless.

Care Instructions: Hand wash in cold water and lay flat to dry. To preserve the delicate ruffle structure, avoid twisting or wringing the hat.
